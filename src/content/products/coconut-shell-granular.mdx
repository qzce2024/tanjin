## Product Overview

Coconut shell granular activated carbon is manufactured from high-quality coconut shells through carbonization and activation processes. It features a highly developed microporous structure, exceptional hardness, and superior adsorption performance.

## Key Features

- **High Iodine Number**: 900-1100 mg/g for excellent adsorption capacity
- **Superior Hardness**: ≥95% ensures durability and long service life
- **Low Ash Content**: ≤8% for high purity applications
- **Renewable Source**: Made from sustainable coconut shells

## Applications

- Drinking water treatment and purification
- Industrial wastewater treatment
- Air purification and odor removal
- Gold recovery in mining operations
- Food and beverage processing
- Pharmaceutical purification
